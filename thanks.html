Staying at Snowbird

Next, you might want to set up a room at the hotel. The conference is in the Cliff Lodge at Snowbird, and you can stay in the same building for just $89 a night! Book your reservation at www.snowbird.com by clicking the "Buy Online" tab located in the far right of the black bar menu, and choosing "Lodging" in the drop down menu. Be sure to enter the group code 2BB5Q3 to get the discounted rate. Or you can call 1-800-453-3000 or email lodging@snowbird.com to make a reservation, just be sure to mention you are with "Ruby|Web Conference" to get the discounted rate.

All reservations, whether booking online or calling in, are fully refundable up until 3 days prior to the scheduled arrival date. Inside 3 days of the scheduled arrival date, there is a one night cancel fee.
