Coming September 9-10, 2010

A Ruby conference specific to the web, with a heavy bias towards Rails, Sinatra, Rack, jQuery, HAML, Mustache, Prototype, Scripty, HTML5, CSS3, OAuth, and more! This exciting event is going to be held on September 9-10, 2010 in the beautiful Wasatch mountains of Utah at Snowbird Ski and Summer Resort, birthplace of the Agile Manifesto.

Registration is Open

Registration is finally open and just $179. Registration gets you two days of conference goodness along with breakfast and lunch and extras like a t-shirt. Also, you will be a better Ruby and/or Web developer because of it.

Staying at Snowbird

The conference is in the Cliff Lodge at Snowbird, and you can stay in the same building for just $89 a night! Book your reservation at www.snowbird.com by clicking the "Buy Online" tab located in the far right of the black bar menu, and choosing "Lodging" in the drop down menu. Be sure to enter the group code 2BB5Q3 to get the discounted rate. Or you can call 1-800-453-3000 or email lodging@snowbird.com to make a reservation, just be sure to mention you are with "Ruby|Web Conference" to get the discounted rate.
